[Rosemary Field]

All events take place in the city where Rosem Flowers moved — Silvergrove (St. Louis County, Minnesota, USA). The main character, Rosem Flower, is a young man suffering from a range of mental disorders, including a mild form of schizophrenia and difficulties with controlling his emotions (especially anger). However, neither Rosem nor his family (out of fear of possible misunderstanding and rejection from others) openly share his problems, carefully trying to hide them from everyone. After the move, he decided to somehow start a new life instead of hiding away in his own dream world.
